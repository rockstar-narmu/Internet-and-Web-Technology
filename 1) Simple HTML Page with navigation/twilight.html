The Twilight Series

The Twilight series by Stephenie Meyer is a popular collection of four fantasy romance novels, following the love story between Bella Swan, a teenage girl, and Edward Cullen, a 104-year-old vampire. The books have been immensely successful, with over 160 million copies sold worldwide, and have been adapted into a blockbuster film series. The saga also includes companion novels, a novella, and a reimagined version of the first book from the male protagonist's perspective. The series remains a cultural phenomenon, particularly among young adults.

  • Bella Swan

  • Alice Cullen
